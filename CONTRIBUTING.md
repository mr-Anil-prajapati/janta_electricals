# Contributing

Thank you for helping improve Janta Electricals' online presence.

## Before you contribute

- Use the information in [`data/business.json`](data/business.json) as the business source of truth.
- Do not publish customer contact details, project photos, or claims without the business owner's approval.
- Keep service descriptions clear, accurate, and locally relevant to Bhowra, Dhanbad, Jharkhand.

## Making a change

1. Create a focused branch and describe the reason for the change.
2. Keep business facts consistent across pages and documentation.
3. Test links and the site at desktop and mobile widths.
4. Open a pull request with a concise summary and screenshots for visual changes.

## Reporting an issue

Use the supplied GitHub issue templates for bugs, content corrections, and feature ideas. Never include sensitive customer information in a public issue.
