## About

Janta Electricals provides residential, commercial, and industrial electrical solutions—from electrical repairs and house wiring to solar systems, control panels, PLC programming, and industrial automation. We focus on safe workmanship, quality materials, fair pricing, and dependable service.

## Services

- Electrical repairs, house wiring, inverter and battery installation
- Solar systems, CCTV installation, and TV & DTH setup
- Smart home automation and electrical maintenance
- Control panel manufacturing, HT/LT panels, PLC programming, and industrial automation
